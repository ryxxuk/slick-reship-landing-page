Privacy Policy

Last update: 8 April 2023

1. Introduction

Slick Reship Ltd is committed to protecting the privacy and security of its customers and users of its website, www.slickreship.com (the "Site"). This privacy policy ("Policy") describes how we collect, use, and disclose personal information, and outlines the choices you have regarding such information.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any cookies or information on user sessions ourselves, however, we do use Google Tag Manager to track page visits. Google Tag Manager collects anonymous information about your use of the Site, including the pages you visit, the links you click, and the other actions you take on the Site. This information is used to improve the functionality of the Site and to better understand how users interact with it. For more information on how Google uses this data, please see Google's Privacy Policy (https://policies.google.com/privacy?hl=en-US).

We also collect personal information when you make a purchase on the Site or sign up for a membership through Whop. We use Stripe (available at https://stripe.com/en-gb-us/privacy) for payment processing, Stripe will collect your name, email address, billing address, and payment information when you make a purchase. We do not store your payment information on our servers. Whop (available at https://whop.com/v2/privacy.pdf) collects personal information when you sign up for a membership, including your name, email address, and password.

3. Use of Information

We may use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To process your purchases and membership sign-ups;
  • To provide customer support and respond to your inquiries;
  • To improve the functionality of the Site;
  • To communicate with you about our products and services;
  • To comply with legal obligations.

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties, except as necessary to provide our services or as required by law.

4. Disclosure of Information

We may disclose your personal information to third parties in the following circumstances:

  • To service providers who help us provide our services, such as Stripe and Whop;
  • To comply with legal obligations, such as responding to a subpoena or court order;
  • In connection with a merger, acquisition, or other business transaction.
